The digital telephony service we offer is a modern, flexible and efficient solution, based on state of the art integrated digital telephony platform. The service allows the use of a private numbering plan, calling an extension number in the client's network, mail-to-fax, fax-to-mail, voice mail, TDM and VoIP services, etc.
